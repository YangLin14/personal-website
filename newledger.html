NewLedger

A mobile app for tracking and categorizing your expenses your way.

Summary

NewLedger is a mobile application designed to help users track their expenses in a personalized way. Unlike traditional expense tracking apps that come with predefined categories, NewLedger allows users to create and customize their own expense categories, making it easier to organize spending in a way that makes sense to them.

The app features an intuitive interface where users can easily add new expenses, create custom categories, and view their spending patterns through various visualization tools. Users can also set budget limits for different categories and receive notifications when approaching their limits.

Built with Flutter and Firebase, NewLedger ensures a smooth, cross-platform experience while maintaining secure cloud storage for all user data. The app also includes features like expense history, monthly summaries, and the ability to export data for personal analysis.

Currently in development, NewLedger aims to provide a flexible and personalized approach to expense tracking, making financial management more accessible and adaptable to individual needs.

Features

  • Custom category creation and management
  • Real-time expense tracking
  • Personalized budget settings
  • Expense analytics and visualizations
  • Cloud data synchronization
  • Cross-platform compatibility
