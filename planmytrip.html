Summary

Introducing ListVaults—the ultimate tool for organizing and tracking your video library. Whether you're into movies, TV shows, or web series, ListVaults allows you to create personalized lists where you can store everything you need to know about your videos.

Add details like the Title, Genre, Episodes, Priority (your personal rating out of 10), Release Date, and Notes. You can even customize categories to match your unique needs. Each list is divided into "To-Watch" and "Watched" sections, so you can easily manage what you have seen and what is next on your list. Moving items between these sections is a breeze, and you can edit any entry whenever you like.

Getting started is easy—just sign up with your name, and your journey begins. With secure cloud storage powered by Firebase, your lists are always safe and accessible, even if you close your browser and return later. ListVaults is designed to make managing your video collection simple, efficient, and enjoyable. Start organizing your videos your way, today.
