Week 2 in Headstarter

The second week of Headstarter.

Summary

On the second week of Headstarter, I started to connect more with my group members on working on the project called Bideo. Bideo, a cutting-edge volumetric video player for VRChat, seamlessly streams stunning holograms with minimal bandwidth, transforming virtual spaces into mesmerizing, interactive 3D environments. Additionally, through the guidance of an experienced group member well-versed in networking and meeting facilitation, I gained valuable insights into effective performance and expectations for real-world stand-up meetings.

In addition to the group project, I also worked on my personal project ListVaults, a place for the users to keep track of their watch lists.
